This here is an app that can remind you when to connect with people at various times of day.
There are 6 times of day to choose from:
Morning
Mid-morning
Afternoon
Afternoon snack time
Dinner
After Dinner

As well as a reset button to clear your input text.

Input one of these and you'll be suggested a spark that tells you different ways of connecting of connecting with people in
your life.
